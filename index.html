4-Phase Expansion Blueprint

  1. Phase 1: Stabilization & Data

    Strengthen single-stall sales (60+ suits/day) and gather essential, replicable market data from the core Lahore bazaar.

  2. Phase 2: Market Expansion

    Expand to new markets in nearby high-footfall areas using initial profits to fund additional stock and setup.

  3. Phase 3: Replication & Staffing

    Recruit and train reliable sellers to manage the replicated model in 3–5 markets, establishing regional micro-hubs.

    Centralize stock purchasing to secure higher volume discounts, reducing per-unit cost and significantly increasing the profit margin beyond 100%.
